About the Amusement Park

The Dream of Human/Bloodfiend Co-existance

Staffed Entirely by the Manchegan Family Bloodfiends, La Manchaland Aims to Achieve its Founder's Dream of Humans and Bloodfiends Co-existing Peacefully.

The Park has Many Attractions, From Games and Rides to Elaborate Locales and a Carnival Parade!

3 Areas of Fun

The Park is Separated into 3 Different Areas with Their Own Attractions!

The Parade of Wonders

Guests can Dress Up and Frolick at Area 3 When the Parade Starts!

Donate Blood for Trinkets

Guests can Donate Blood Around the Park's Attractions for Specific Goodies!

Main Attractions

Area 1 - Fantasy Blood-Shooting Range

Part Shooting Gallery Part Narration Skit, This Attraction Tells the Tale of How La Manchaland Came to Be!

Area 2 - Haunted House Bloody Mary

A Haunted House with Mystery Solution Elements, Find the Exit While Avoiding the Ghosts!

Area 3 - The Carnival

This Area House Most of the Park's Standard Attractions, from 19:30 to 20:30 the Parade Begins!
